I am a first year PhD Student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of California, Davis, working on Natural Lanuage Processing and advised by Prof.Muhao Chen.

I am driven by the aspiration to develop intelligent systems capable of seamless interactions with humans. Currently I am working on improving language model reasoning efficiency and enhancing reasoning generalization.
